About

Counterrevolutionary Concepts is a small creative development studio committed to cultural renewal through traditional principles. We build apps, content, and tools that reconnect people with natural rhythm, historical insight, and spiritual structure.

Current Project

Our flagship application, Roman Solar Time, revives the ancient tradition of solar-based timekeeping. It partitions each day into canonical hours and vigils, rooted in both Roman and monastic practice.
