App Privacy Policy – Roman Solar Time

Roman Solar Time for iOS uses location data solely on-device to calculate solar time. Your precise location is never required – a 3 km radius approximation is sufficient.

All location-based calculations are performed locally on your device. No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted by the app. We do not use any third-party analytics, ad tracking, or external servers.

If you choose to submit a bug report from within the app, you may optionally share a diagnostic log or screenshot that includes your current solar location or other derived information. This data is used solely for troubleshooting and will never be shared with third parties. You are advised to confirm (via the settings menu in the Roman Solar Time app, or via Location Services in your device's Settings app) that you are sharing approximate, not precise, location before lodging a bug report.

If you choose to enable optional features (e.g. solar minute display), this may involve a one-time in-app purchase. All transactions are processed securely via Apple’s App Store and are not visible to us.
